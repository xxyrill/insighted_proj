Purpose of the Software

Our software, Mapúa insightED, is a data analytics solution designed to enhance educational decision-making at Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao. By leveraging advanced data analytics techniques, our platform aims to provide valuable insights that help educators and administrators make informed decisions to improve the educational experience and outcomes for students.

Our goal is to empower educational institutions with the tools they need to drive continuous improvement and achieve excellence in education.
